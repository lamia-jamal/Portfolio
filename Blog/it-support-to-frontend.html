From IT Support to Frontend Development: What My Experience Taught Me

May 2026 · 5 min read

My journey into frontend development started from a technical support background working with LMS and web-based systems.

Over time, I became increasingly interested in frontend interfaces, responsive layouts, and how user experience impacts learning platforms.

Skills I Developed Along the Way

  • HTML and CSS
  • Responsive web design
  • WordPress and LearnDash customization
  • Troubleshooting frontend issues
  • User-focused problem solving

Working on real production systems taught me practical problem-solving skills that continue to shape my frontend development approach today.

I believe combining technical support experience with frontend development creates a stronger understanding of how users interact with digital systems.
